Is Craft Care for me?

Websites are a big investment and the ongoing technical maintenance can be time consuming. Craft Care is a solution for businesses running a Craft website without an in-house developer or designated day-to-day development partner.

With Craft Care you’re able to focus on your content while we ensure your site is safe and secure and functioning the way it should. Do you have a question or need a quick front end fix? Craft Care provides the peace of mind knowing that questions will be answered within one business day, guaranteed and small changes can be made quickly. Do you have a website that needs more extensive technical support?

With our tiered plans, you can pick the level of support that suits your business. Think of Craft Care as an extension of your technical team.

How does Craft Care work?

Craft Care is a service provided by Craft team members to help keep your website running smoothly. Our in-house team will ensure that your site is running on the most recent version of Craft and is backed up and secure. They will also provide technical support and be on-hand for any small changes that you need made to your site.

Are custom Craft Care plans available?

If you or your client have a specific set of support needs we are happy to have a conversation. We’ve worked hard to create packages that offer comprehensive support at a reasonable price - but value our users input and questions. Email us at … to start the conversation.

As an agency can I offer Craft Care to my clients?

Absolutely! Craft Care is meant to supplement agency / client relationships by providing technical support for Craft users in addition to their agency partnerships.
